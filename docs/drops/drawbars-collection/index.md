## Background

**drawbars** participated in OTP22 during the original run (2012–2016) and has continued in the revival. This section documents items held directly by drawbars, based on first-hand knowledge.

## Sebastopol, CA Drop (October 22, 2013)

**Source:** `2029993335 16041`
**Date discovered:** 2013-10-16
**Date found:** 2013-10-22

### Items

**Cleveland Direct Calling Card #2**
- Physical prepaid calling card with PIN
- Unlocks "class six" numbers via +1-412-888-0004
- This is the second Cleveland Direct card documented in the game's history (Smugtrio held #1 from the July 2013 Playa del Rey drop)
- Limited minutes like a real prepaid card

**Note**
- Text: *"AUTHENTIC NIGHTLIGHT FOR FUTURE USE"*
- The meaning of "AUTHENTIC NIGHTLIGHT" remains cryptic. Possible interpretations: a callsign, an operational code, a literal instruction, or flavor text.

## Rohnert Park, CA Drop (March 25, 2014)

**Source:** `2029993335`
**Date discovered:** 2014-03-25
**Date found:** 2014-03-25

### Items

**FUNCTIONAL TEST FOR AS36**
- Printed document
- AS36 = "Alpha Sigma 36," the remote field installation central to the original run's operational arc
- A battery/solar/radio unit that went through tape log buffer overflow issues in 2014

**FUNCTIONAL TEST RESULT REPORT for AS36**
- Companion document to the above
- Held by drawbars

These two documents are among the only physical documentation of the AS36 system's hardware and testing procedures in player hands.
