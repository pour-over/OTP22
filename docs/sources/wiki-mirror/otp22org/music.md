## Background

Around **May 2024**, Emtanon recruited a 1980s pop cover band to become, unbeknownst to them, the "Marine Org shadow ensemble." The band was in dire financial straits, reportedly having only **€73.22** in their bank account. After signing their lives away, they were christened **OTP22** and taken to Gough Island to record original music with lyrics based on the characters.

> The band's music makes at least partial use of AI.

The band eventually realized their situation and began pushing back — demanding release of their non-propaganda songs as well (see Court House Dolphin, Follow Her Not).

### Court House Dolphin

**Release date:** June 6, 2024
**Source:** https://otp22.org/doku.php?id=court_house_dolphin

While the band was hired to produce Emtanon propaganda music (like "We Promise You"), **they demanded release of their other songs as well**. This demand was documented in XORFS message 22045. Court House Dolphin is one of the non-propaganda releases.

The title references **Cape May Court House, NJ** — the location of Emtanon's US Field Agent Coordinator, Debbie Wright.

**Available on:** Streaming services.

## In-Universe Context

The band "OTP22" is an unwitting tool of Emtanon's propaganda apparatus. Their musical output serves two functions:
1. **Propaganda** (We Promise You): Glorifies J and the WORM mission
2. **Subversive** (Court House Dolphin, Follow Her Not): Songs the band pushed to release over Emtanon's objections

Robby's involvement in tipping off the community about Follow Her Not suggests he has contacts with or knowledge of the band's situation — consistent with his role as an insider with broad visibility into Emtanon operations.
