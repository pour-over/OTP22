## Islands

### Bouvet Island

Bouvet Island (Bouvetøya) is a subantarctic volcanic island in the South Atlantic Ocean, and the world's most remote island. It is administered by Norway as a nature reserve.

Emtanon maintains a presence here, likely related to their remote island operations network.

**Wikipedia:** https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bouvet_Island

---

### Gough Island

Gough Island is a remote island in the South Atlantic, part of the UK Overseas Territory of Saint Helena, Ascension and Tristan da Cunha.

Emtanon operates a **weather station** here as cover for their South Atlantic operations. Key events at Gough Island:
- Felicity Love was an intern at the weather station in May–June 2023 before escaping
- The [Gough Ceremony](events.md#gough-ceremony) took place here on September 24, 2023
- The band "OTP22" was taken here to record their music

**Wikipedia:** https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gough_Island

---

### Leskov Island

Leskov Island is one of the South Sandwich Islands in the South Atlantic.
