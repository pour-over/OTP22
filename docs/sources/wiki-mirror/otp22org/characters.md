# Characters — otp22.org Wiki Mirror

**Source:** https://otp22.org/doku.php?id=characters

OTP22 features dozens of characters, many known only by their initials. These are the more prominent personalities in the revival era.

---

## Major Characters

### Dr. J Marvin Blackweather

J is a British man who is the founder and boss of Emtanon, Marine Org, and WORM. He's known for:
- Narcissistic behavior
- Smashing things
- Being bad with technology
- Immense wealth, constantly shown off (typical meal: caviar, truffles, wagyu)
- Occasional use of archaic words from early 20th century — *he might be a vampire*

**Notable:** [JSay tool](https://otp22.org/tools/jsay.html) archives J's greatest hits.

---

### Felicity Love

**Full page:** https://otp22.org/doku.php?id=characters:felicity_love

Felicity is an American woman from Ocracoke, North Carolina. She was an **intern at the Gough Island weather station** during May–June 2023, from which she miraculously escaped. With community aid via messages on Instagram, she evaded capture by Marine Org mercenaries for months.

**Key timeline:**
- Spring 2023: Hired by Emtanon, flew to Gough Island
- June 2023: Escaped the island
- July 2023: On the run — placed Cape May dead drop
- August–October 2023: Evading Marine Org pursuit
- November 2023: Manhunt canceled; has been mostly silent since

**Felicity's diary:** A full personal diary documenting her journey from innocent intern to fugitive — see [Special Content](special-content.md#felicitys-diary).
**Felicity's voicemail:** SSTV images and drop locations broadcast on her voicemail number — see [Special Content](special-content.md#felicitys-voicemail).

### Jeff A.

**Full page:** https://otp22.org/doku.php?id=characters:jeff_a

Jeff is a Emtanon drop agent and insider who defected. He:
- Placed multiple dead drops in his role as a drop agent (Riverside, Tuktoyaktuk, Slide Cemetery, Federal Way)
- Hid the Federal Way drop coordinates by writing them on Felicity's hand during a hiking date
- Provided community with FACILITY semaphore passwords to free prisoners
- Appears to be based in Riverside, California (his "hometown")

---

### Maria Peters

**Full page:** https://otp22.org/doku.php?id=characters:maria_peters

Maria Peters is an Emtanon field operative who occasionally acts as a drop agent. She:
- Was dispatched to place the Irving drop (November 2024) after a local agent quit to work at Starbucks
- Leaked internal data that was found in the El Paso drop (images: Maria_1.jpg through Maria_4.jpg)
- Announced drop location casually on Slate: `OK ITS AT 32.8210 -97.0093 *_*`

---

### Tala Torres

Tala is a **Filipino woman** who is J's companion. Background:
- J met her during a vacation on Ibiza in August 2023
- J takes her on all his travels, going to great lengths to accommodate her
- Massive reported age gap between them
- **Tala's ultimate plan is to take over Emtanon**

## Minor Characters

### AIR1

Bombardier Global 6500 jet (one of two Marine Org aircraft). Also appears as an Iris talk group (`112`).

### Desiree Martin

Emtanon operative who deleted SSTV images from Felicity's voicemail to obscure drop locations — specifically removed detailed location photos for the Riverside drop, leaving only two images.

---

### Esprit Boursicot

Agent with Iris talk group `16001`. Minor operational character.

---

### MO5

Ship (formerly the role of MO3). The crew of MO5 defected during the Operation Spice aftermath. Now under new management — talk groups `201` and `555`.

### Morgan "The Edge" Fellman

Minor character. The nickname "The Edge" suggests a field operations role.

### Robert Bramble ("Robby")

Robert Bramble is the **revival-era whistleblower** analogous to the original-run "Robby" insider. He:
- Communicates via [XORFS](messages.md#xorfs) messages
- Hinted at the release of the band's song "Follow Her Not" in XORFS message 22475: `ALSO, THE BAND GOT THEM TO PUBLISH THIS SUPER CREEPY SONG A WHILE BACK. […] I THINK IT WILL HIT STREAMING SOON.`
- Continuation of the "Robby/Robert" insider character from the original run who used the Robbybox (50565732)
