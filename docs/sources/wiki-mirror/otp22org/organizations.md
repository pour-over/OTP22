## Emtanon

[Emtanon Ltd](https://emtanon.com) is a British corporation founded in **1975**. Active in logistics, hardware, and software development, they are a front company for the illegal activities of Marine Org and WORM.

Their operations include:
- Contract work: weather stations, construction of amusement parks, sales of slot machines
- Marketing backdoored IoT solutions to manufacturers of home appliances
- Retaining obsolete technology dating back to the 1970s (indefinitely)
- Books and letters awkwardly refer to calendar years as "time periods"

**Key personnel:**
- Dr. J Marvin Blackweather — founder and boss
- Debbie Wright — Field Agent Coordinator, based in Cape May Court House, NJ
- Debra Fisher — Chief Finance Manager (has dirt on her in `insurance.bin`)

## WORM

WORM (also MLPL in original-run) is the clandestine criminal organization operating under Emtanon's cover. Their activities include:
- Melting polar ice caps (using the "Melter fleet")
- Stealing seed vaults
- Operating mercenary teams
- Working with rogue governments
- Running global dead drop and agent networks

In-universe, players act as counter-operatives attempting to stop WORM by intercepting dead drops, decoding communications, and disrupting operations.
