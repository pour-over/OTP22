## About the Robbybox

Agent code **50565732** was an internal voicemail system discovered and repurposed by "Robby" (Robert), a rogue insider employee of the MLPL organization. He described it as:

> "This is routed throughout multiple systems into something that isn't used any more. I made this goofing around one day."

Robby used this channel to communicate with OTP22 community members/researchers, revealing insider knowledge about the organization's systems, operations, and vulnerabilities. He was hunted by J's operatives throughout late 2013 after escaping.

**Identity:** Robert (last name unknown). References to "a horse" suggest a callsign or distinguishing feature. Signs messages "Aloha" (Hawaiian greeting) and later "Aloha and Palaam" (Tagalog farewell). Spent time in Hawaii, traveled internationally.
