# Characters

*All known characters across both eras of OTP22.*

---

## Original Run (2012–2016)

### J
The primary authority figure. High-ranking organizational leader, often terse and demanding. Sends orders to field agents and operational coordinators. Known for emotional outbursts, e.g. *"I CANT BELIEVE YOU LET THAT STUPID LITTLE BASTARD GET AWAY"* (Oct 2013, re: Robby's escape). References to J's demands appear throughout the 18xxx OTP message archive. In the revival, J is fully named as **Dr. J Marvin Blackweather** — a narcissistic, wealthy British man, founder of Emtanon, Marine Org, and WORM.

### AI Bouerges
Primary field agent. Code: `16041`.

- **DOB:** 06/21/1984
- **Height:** 157 cm
- **Weight:** 49 kg
- **Ethnicity:** Asian
- **COB:** JP (Japan)
- **Base:** Playa del Rey, CA (later Las Vegas, then disappeared)

The most active agent in the original run. Profile revealed 2012-11-24. Smugtrio drops in 2013 were addressed to AI Bouerges at Playa del Rey. Disappeared mysteriously in spring 2013 — J's frantic message *"SHE IS NOT HERE. WE ARE ALL WAITING. WHERE IS SHE?"* (March 2013) refers to her absence. Her whereabouts remain unresolved in the original run.

Agent voicemail: `16041`. Upload/handshake: `16241`.

### Angela (A)
Operational coordinator. Manages the TRANSP fleet. Active in 2013 standoff messages regarding payload loading timing and satellite imagery concerns. Involved in Melter fleet deployment coordination.

### D
Field operative. On the ground during operations. Active in late 2013 (LONGVAULT operation) and throughout 2014. Referenced in OTP messages as heading to shore locations.

### Robert "Robby" Bramble
Internal employee turned whistleblower. See full profile below (he appears in both eras).

### B01
Field operative in Bulgaria. Present during Dimitrovgrad/Sofia drops, September 2013.

### K
Another operator. Referenced in OTP messages.

### PETR
Field agent. Active during late 2013 standoff — delivers items to TRANSP.

## Robert "Robby" Bramble

*Appears in both original run (as whistleblower) and revival (as Emtanon ITOPS staff member)*

### Original Run
Robby was an internal Emtanon employee with system access. He discovered the Robbybox voicemail system (`50565732`) on September 22, 2013, and began using it to communicate with OTP22 players.

His first message: *"I made this goofing around one day. Hopefully my access still works."*

Over the following weeks, he:
- Offered to share information of "great interest"
- Explained multiple communication channels (X.25, TDD, ABISM, HF, Zeus)
- Fled the organization with a Green Book and materials
- Evaded agents at three airports in October 2013 (using dummy tickets)
- Prompted J's furious response: *"I CANT BELIEVE YOU LET THAT STUPID LITTLE BASTARD GET AWAY"*
- Continued communicating from hiding: *"Do you have the WORM documents and the audio CDs? Aloha."* (Nov 2013)

Robby spent time in Hawaii ("Aloha" is his greeting). His status at the end of the original run is unknown.

### Revival
Robert "Robby" Bramble appears as an Emtanon ITOPS team member based in Liberty, Kentucky. He manages the AS32 system. It is unclear whether this is a reconciliation with the organization, a different timeline, or whether the revival treats the original run as backstory.

## Revival Era Characters (2023–present)

### Dr. J Marvin Blackweather ("J")
**Origin:** British
**Role:** Founder and head of Emtanon Ltd, Marine Org, and WORM
**Personality:** Narcissistic, demanding, wealthy. Requested a red terminal because green was "stupid" (message 18349). Makes impulsive decisions. Companion: Tala Torres (since August 2023, Ibiza).

Notable quotes sourced from JSay tool at otp22.org/tools/.

### Felicity Love
**Origin:** Ocracoke, North Carolina, USA
**Status:** Fugitive / counter-operative

Intern at Gough Island weather station who discovered Emtanon's criminal operations. Escaped to Brazil aboard a Doritos delivery ship. Has been evading Marine Org manhunt. Has Instagram (@felicitylove97), a personal diary, and a voicemail system. Key contact for players seeking Path Controller access codes.

### Jeff A.
**Origin:** Riverside, Iowa, USA
**Status:** Missing since January 2024

Inside man who was plotting to bring down Emtanon from within. Disappeared January 2024. Status unknown. His Iowa hometown connects to the revival-era Iowa drop.

### Maria Peters
**Role:** Emtanon computer security expert
**Affiliation:** Known troublemaker within Emtanon

Handler for Operation Spice (Lima, Peru, January 2024). The operation failed; Maria mishandled it. Crew was captured by a Peruvian cartel. She remains within Emtanon.

### Morgan "The Edge" Fellman
**Status:** Freed — former FACILITY prisoner

Kidnapped by Marine Org in 2022 and imprisoned on the ship FACILITY. Led the **FACILITY Redemption** mutiny in February 2024 — players helped by flipping Path Controller semaphore bytes from `0xFF` to `0x00`. Detainees took control of FACILITY after killing bridge crew with chemical weapons. Now sailing in the Pacific.

### Tala Torres
Filipino woman. J's companion since August 2023 Ibiza vacation. Role in broader narrative unclear.

### MO5
Crew member on Marine Org vessel MO5. Secretly friendly to counter-operatives. Defected during Operation Spice fallout. Worked with AIR1 to take Aqua Terminal (Hilo) offline on February 21, 2024 to hide evidence.

### AIR1
Pilot of Emtanon aircraft AIR1 (modified Bombardier Global 6500). Secretly part of the plot to bring down Emtanon. Coordinated with MO5 on the Aqua Terminal operation.

### Esprit Boursicot
Works at Emtanon HQ (The Rock, Gibraltar). Supervises operations, tests terminals. Uses the Green Terminal (Room 101). Reachable on Iris talk group `16001`.

### Desiree Martin
Senior Project Manager. Based on Gough Island weather station. Reachable on Iris talk group `17001`.

### Robert "Robby" Bramble (Revival)
Emtanon ITOPS team. Based in Liberty, Kentucky. Manages the AS32 system. Reachable on Iris network.

### Debbie Wright
Field Agent Coordinator. Located in Cape May Court House, NJ. Manages the postal mail operation. PO Box: DROP AGENT CATALOG, 615 US-9 S #172, CMCH, NJ 08210.

### Debra Fisher
Chief Finance Manager. Referenced in message 18438 which hints that `insurance.bin` (from El Paso drop) contains compromising information about her.
