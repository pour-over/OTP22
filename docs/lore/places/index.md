# Places

*All known locations in the OTP22 universe, both in-game and real-world drop sites.*

---

## In-Game Locations

### The Rock (Gibraltar)
**Emtanon HQ.** The primary facility housing:
- **Green Terminal** — Room 101 (Esprit Boursicot's workspace)
- **Amber Terminal** — Room 104 (auxiliary/visitor terminal)
- **Red Terminal** — J's personal terminal (requested red because green was "stupid")

Iris groups: Green Terminal = `16009`, Amber Terminal = `16921`.

### Gough Island
South Atlantic weather station. Coordinates: approximately 40°21'S, 9°53'W.
- Felicity Love worked here as an intern before discovering Emtanon's operations
- Site of the Gough Ceremony (September 24, 2023)
- Training facility for agents
- Desiree Martin is Senior Project Manager here (Iris: `17001`)

### Hilo, Hawaii
Pacific Command. Houses the **Aqua Terminal** (Room 101, Iris: `19001`). Key role in Operation Spice planning and FACILITY Redemption. Taken offline February 21, 2024; restored June 7, 2024.

### Bouvet Island
Subantarctic island. 54°26'S, 3°24'E. One of the most remote places on Earth — Norwegian territory, no permanent inhabitants. North Korean missiles acquired here January 2024; construction underway since March 2024. Purpose unknown.

### Leskov Island
South Atlantic. Referenced in SHIP coordinate messages.

### Exeter, UK
UK HQ of Emtanon.

### Iqaluit, Canada
Field office. Phone: +1 (867) 794-0033. Also: AS33 mirror at +1 (867) 794-0004.

### CMCH, NJ (Cape May Court House)
Location of Debbie Wright, Field Agent Coordinator. Real PO Box:
```
DROP AGENT CATALOG
615 US-9 S #172
CMCH, NJ 08210
```

### Liberty, Kentucky
Base of Robert "Robby" Bramble, ITOPS. Manages AS32.

### Svalbard / Longyearbyen, Norway
**Final assembly point** for original run components. Referenced in the October 2012 broadcast: *"PROCEED TO SVALBARD VAULT FOR FINAL ASSEMBLY."* Also known as the Svalbard Global Seed Vault — a real location storing seeds from worldwide crops. WORM's seed vault infiltration plan targeted this facility.

### Lima, Peru
Site of Operation Spice (January 2024). WORM operation failed; crew captured by Peruvian cartel.

### Ocracoke, North Carolina
Felicity Love's hometown. A small barrier island community.

### Riverside, Iowa
Jeff A.'s hometown. Also related to an Iowa dead drop (~2025).

## Significant Original Run Phone Locations

- **Fort Lauderdale Airport** — TDD payphone, number `954-359-0332` (AT&T). Discovered when L0j1k had an airport worker dial it.
- **Bangkok phone booth** — DVD affixed to ceiling. GPS coordinates from agent system led there.
- **Arctic/Bering Sea** — TRANSP fleet operational area; no player physically reached.
- **Svalbard** — Destination for components; no player physically reached.
