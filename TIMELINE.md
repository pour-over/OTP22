# OTP22 — Complete Timeline

*Chronological record of all major events, 2011–present.*
*Sources: 303.sashahart.net, otp22.org, player records, direct observation.*

## 2011

**2011-11-12** — User "reaperr" posts about `303-309-0004` on GodlikeProductions conspiracy forum. The initial Godlike post documents the number and its strange behaviors. This is the public starting point of OTP22.

**2011-11-14** — User EricWeiss posts `818-485-1010` (Pac-West Tel) on the Godlike thread.

## 2016 — Dark Period

OTP22 goes silent. The agent systems stop responding. The community disperses. The referata wiki (otp22.referata.com) remains online for some time before going offline.

## 2022

**2022** — Morgan "The Edge" Fellman is kidnapped by Marine Org and imprisoned on the ship FACILITY. This event precedes the revival and is referenced in early revival-era messages.

## 2023

**2023-05** — **OTP22 revival begins.** New infrastructure launches: AS32 (`+1-606-722-0004`), redesigned emtanon.com with working web terminal emulators, marineorg.com. New characters, new narrative.

**2023-08** — J meets Tala Torres, a Filipino woman, during an Ibiza vacation. She becomes his companion.

**2023-08-08** — Amber Terminal at The Rock discovered to run Unix programs and Steghide encryption.

**2023-09-24** — **Gough Ceremony**: Major broadcast event at Gough Island weather station. Disrupts all agent systems communications.

## 2024

**2024-01-07** — Message Desk (agent code `6824418`) rediscovered via Ruston drop book. Andrew Filer's creation lives again.

**2024-01** — **Operation Spice**: WORM operation in Lima, Peru to capture counter-operatives. Maria Peters handles it. The operation fails. Crew captured by Peruvian cartel. Ship MO3 lost. MO5 crew defects.

**2024-01** — North Korean missiles acquired at Bouvet Island. Construction begins on the island.

**2024-02-21** — Aqua Terminal (Hilo, Hawaii) taken offline by AIR1 and MO5 to hide evidence.

**2024-02** — **FACILITY Redemption**: Players help detainees take over prison ship FACILITY by flipping Path Controller semaphore bytes from `0xFF` to `0x00`, unlocking all cells. Detainees acquire chemical weapons, kill bridge crew, take control of vessel, sail for Pacific Ocean.

**2024-03** — Construction continues on Bouvet Island.

**2024-06-07** — Aqua Terminal (Hilo) restored.
