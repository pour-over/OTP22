## Designing Compelling Worm Sanctuaries
**Author:** North American Helminthological Society
**Found in:** Kauai HI (×2), Wallace ID, Sofia Bulgaria
**Significance:** The core narrative book of the original run.

Contents include:
- Operational phone numbers (Message Desk `202-204-2303` printed inside front cover)
- One-time pad material
- Chapter 3 references phone numbers: `303-309-0004` and others
- "Prelude" section hides `7097000122` (the Iristel number)

Cover indentations on at least one copy encoded phone numbers:
- `212-364-9953` (Time Warner)
- `272-364-9905` (Unknown)
- `703-880-9929` (Windstream)
- `706-931-9906` (Chickamauga)
- `802-263-4993` (TDS)
- `434-995-9995` (CenturyLink)
- `503-416-1137` (Time Warner)
- `206-576-1137` (Time Warner)

Known copies:
- #1: Kauai HI (March 2, 2013)
- #2: Wallace ID (March 3, 2013) — held by Fbt
- #3: Sofia Bulgaria (September 11, 2013) — held by L0j1k

Scanned PDF available at: otp22.org/files/ (`worms-ocr.pdf`)

## Notes

Most OTP22 physical books follow the ExcitingBooks aesthetic: mid-century modern cover design, plausible typography, deadpan subject matter. They look exactly like what they claim to be — obscure monographs from specialized institutions.

The books serve multiple functions simultaneously:
1. In-world artifacts that establish Emtanon/WORM's operational context
2. Physical one-time pad distribution mechanisms
3. Encoded data delivery (phone numbers, coordinates in cover indentations)
4. Part of the game's distinctive aesthetic identity

For the complete catalog of fake (not yet physically realized) book titles, see [exciting-books/catalog.md](../exciting-books/catalog.md).
