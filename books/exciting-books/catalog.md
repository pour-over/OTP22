## About ExcitingBooks

Andrew Filer ran @excitingbooks (Twitter, joined December 2013, 1,144 posts, 1,199 followers) and a companion Tumblr. Every cover is designed with precision — correct typography, period-appropriate layout, plausible publisher imprints, real-sounding authors. They could exist. In the world of OTP22, they do.

Covers that appear in physical drops are marked with a drop indicator.
