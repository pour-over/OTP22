### 303-309-0004 — West / Frederica
**Carrier:** Level 3 Communications
**Status:** Inactive (original run)

The original number. First documented on GodlikeProductions, November 12, 2011 by user "reaperr." The starting point of OTP22. Sometimes called "Frederica" or "West." Accepted DTMF agent codes and returned encoded messages, OTP sequences, ABISM tones, SSTV images, and coordinates.

## Named Systems

### 412-888-0004 — Cleveland Direct / Hampton
**Carrier:** Level 3 Communications

A "calling card" system for dialing "class six" telephone numbers — numbers not accessible from ordinary phone lines. Requires a PIN from a physical Cleveland Direct calling card, obtained via postal mail or in physical drops.

Calling cards were found in:
- Smugtrio #2, Playa del Rey CA (July 30, 2013) — Card #1
- Sebastopol, CA (October 22, 2013) — Card #2, held by **drawbars**. Note: *"AUTHENTIC NIGHTLIGHT FOR FUTURE USE"*

References:
- 2013-06-23 and later: Referenced on `2029993335 16041` and other codes
- 2013-07-03: L0j1k appended 0004 to `412888` mentioned on message 18111
- 2013-07-30: Printed on Smugtrio's Cleveland Direct calling card
- 2013-09-06: DTMF tones from Bulgaria video (2:26–2:33)

## Infrastructure Analysis

The overwhelming pattern: nearly every number is provisioned on **Level 3 Communications** (now Lumen Technologies) infrastructure, and nearly every Level 3 number ends in `0004`.

This is not coincidental. Provisioning this many Level 3 DIDs across dozens of area codes — all with the same last four digits — requires either:
1. Wholesale access to Level 3's SIP trunking platform, or
2. Deep operational knowledge of how to request specific DIDs from Level 3 resellers

Both scenarios point to someone with professional telecom experience. Level 3 was the backbone carrier that sophisticated voice-over-IP operators used in the 2010s. The phone phreaking and hacker community had strong awareness of Level 3's infrastructure.

For analysis of the production team hypothesis, see: [meta/shadytel-connection.md](../../../meta/shadytel-connection.md)
