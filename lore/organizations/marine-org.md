# Marine Org

*J's extrajudicial paramilitary maritime force.*

---

## Overview

**Marine Org** is one of Dr. J Marvin Blackweather's three organizations. It operates as a private paramilitary navy and air force, accountable to no government. Its recruitment materials present it as a legitimate maritime security organization; in reality it operates outside all legal frameworks.

**Website:** https://marineorg.com (includes YouTube recruitment channel)

**Recruitment terms:**
- 6-year minimum conscription
- NDA requirements
- No details on termination provisions

## Key Events

### FACILITY Imprisonment (2022–2024)
Marine Org kidnapped Morgan "The Edge" Fellman in 2022 and imprisoned him aboard FACILITY. Multiple other detainees were also held on the ship.

### Operation Spice (January 2024)
Marine Org operation in Lima, Peru targeting counter-operatives. Handler: Maria Peters. Outcome: catastrophic failure. MO3 lost, crew captured by Peruvian cartel. MO5 crew defected.

### FACILITY Redemption (February 2024)
Players assisted detainees in taking over FACILITY by providing Path Controller access to flip semaphore bytes:
- All cell locks: `0xFF` → `0x00` (unlock)
- Detainees acquired chemical weapons and used them on bridge crew
- Detainees took control; FACILITY sailed for Pacific Ocean
- The Edge was freed

### Bouvet Island Construction (January 2024–present)
North Korean missiles acquired at Bouvet Island (subantarctic). Construction has been underway since March 2024. Purpose unclear.
